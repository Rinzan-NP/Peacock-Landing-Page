# Peacock Electro-Mechanical Works L.L.C - Full Company Profile

## 🏢 Executive Overview
**Peacock Electro-Mechanical Works L.L.C** is a premier "Total Outsourcing Solutions" provider based in Dubai, UAE, with a global reach through its Indian subsidiary, **Peacock Resources LLP**. Established in 2005, the company has built a reputation for delivering high-quality engineering, technical manpower, and project support services across the Middle East.

### 🎯 Mission & Philosophy
> "To provide high-quality outsourcing services that empower our customers to achieve their business objectives in a faster, easier, and most cost-effective way."

Peacock operates on a "Client First" philosophy, ensuring that engineering solutions are not only technical accurate but also economically viable and safety-compliant.

## 🛠️ Core Engineering Divisions

### 1. Primary Engineering Services
Specialized engineering for High Voltage (HV) and Extra High Voltage (EHV) substations:
*   **Substation Design:** Comprehensive layout design for indoor and outdoor substations, including detailed cross-sections.
*   **Calculations:** Substation fault level calculations, sizing of earthing grids, and lightning protection systems.
*   **Component Sizing:** Battery and UPS sizing, CT (Current Transformer) and VT (Voltage Transformer) compatibility checks.
*   **Infrastructure:** Cable sizing, trenching layouts, and containment design.

### 2. Secondary Engineering Services
Focus on control, protection, and automation:
*   **Logic Design:** Control and protection logic diagrams.
*   **Wiring Systems:** Detailed wiring diagrams and termination schedules.
*   **Automation:** Substation Automation System (SAS) and protection interface.
*   **Documentation:** FAT (Factory Acceptance Test) and SAT (Site Acceptance Test) documentation and procedures.

### 3. Engineering Design & Drafting
Leveraging global standards and high-end software:
*   **AutoCAD & MicroStation:** Professional 2D/3D drafting for all engineering disciplines.
*   **Structural Design:** Advanced calculations following International Codes (British, American, Euro) and GASCO DGS.
*   **Conversion:** Scanning and conversion of hardcopy drawings to intelligent softcopy formats.

## 🏗️ Building Information Modeling (BIM)
Peacock is a leader in BIM implementation, offering services from 3D visualization to 7D facility management.

| BIM Level | Focus Area | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **3D BIM** | **Visualization** | 3D coordination, clash detection, and model-based design. |
| **4D BIM** | **Scheduling** | Integration of time-related data for project scheduling and sequencing. |
| **5D BIM** | **Estimation** | Extraction of quantities and cost estimation directly from the model. |
| **6D BIM** | **Sustainability** | Energy analysis and sustainability modeling for green building standards. |
| **7D BIM** | **FM & Lifecycle** | Post-construction facility management and lifecycle data integration. |

### Specialized BIM Applications
*   **Substation Mechanical Modeling:** Modeling of heavy electrical equipment and support structures.
*   **MEP Coordination:** Virtual design and construction for Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing.
*   **GIS Modeling:** Geographic Information Systems integration for infrastructure projects.
